How Swiss Roll Was Born

It started with a bad joke at a cafÃ© in Plainpalais.

"You know what Geneva needs?" said Dr. Sophie Mercier to her colleague over coffee. "A physio clinic that doesn't feel like a hospital. Somewhere people actually want to go."

"Like a bakery?" joked Dr. Antoine Dubois.

"Exactly like a bakery. But instead of pastries, we roll out your pain."

They laughed. Then they looked at each other. Then they stopped laughing and started planning.

Three months later, Swiss Roll Physio opened its doors. The name stuck. The concept worked. Patients loved it. Even the serious ones.

Today, we're Geneva's favorite physiotherapy clinic (self-proclaimed, but also backed by actual patient reviews and several Google stars). We combine world-class treatment with a sense of humor, because healing shouldn't be grim.
